Flagship Venture

Hatter Consulting

Stetson University's first undergraduate consulting club, founded to bridge the gap between academic instruction and professional consulting practice.

“When I looked around campus, I kept hitting the same wall: there was no place for business students to practice thinking like consultants. No structured experience. No client work. So I built it.”

Hatter Consulting delivers affordable strategic advisory to DeLand and Central Florida businesses, nonprofits, and entrepreneurs, while giving students the real-world reps the classroom can't.

The model: four pillars, one purpose: real consulting experience.

📋

Client Engagements

Teams of 3–5 students deliver strategy memos, market research, and operational recommendations for local businesses and nonprofits.

🧠

Skills Workshops

Monthly sessions on consulting frameworks, case analysis, financial modeling, and presentation skills.

🎤

Speaker Series

Each semester, professionals from consulting, entrepreneurship, and finance share insights and connect members to the industry.

🏆

Case Competitions

Structured prep for regional and national case competitions, putting Stetson students on the map.
