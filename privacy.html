Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This website (michaelyadlosky.com) is the personal portfolio of Michael Yadlosky. This page explains what information is collected when you visit or contact me, and how it is used.

Information I collect

  • Contact form: If you use the inquiry form, I collect the name, email address, and message you provide, so that I can read and respond to you.
  • Basic technical data: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may automatically log standard information such as your IP address and browser type for security and operation. I do not control or use that data directly.

This site does not use cookies, advertising, or analytics tracking.

How your information is used

  • I use the details you submit only to respond to your inquiry and to follow up with you.
  • I do not sell, rent, or share your information for marketing.

Consent

By submitting the contact form, you consent to the collection and use of the information you provide as described in this policy, so that I can respond to your inquiry. You can withdraw your consent at any time by emailing me to request deletion.

Third-party services

To function, this site relies on:

  • FormSubmit processes contact-form submissions and delivers them to my email. Your form data passes through their service to reach me.
  • GitHub Pages hosts this website.

These providers handle data under their own privacy policies.

Data retention

I keep the messages you send for as long as needed to respond and maintain our correspondence. You can ask me to delete your information at any time.

Your choices

You can email me at myadlosky@stetson.edu to ask what information I have, request a correction, or request deletion.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general, professional audience and is not directed at children. I do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through this site, please email me and I will delete it.

Changes to this policy

I may update this policy as the site evolves. The current version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

This policy is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.
